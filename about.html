Offline Emergency Knowledge Search

The Mission

The JIC project delivers a self-contained LLM-powered conversational search engine over curated emergency survival PDFs that works without the Internet.

This includes survival guides, medical references, agricultural know-how, engineering resources, and broader educational materials. If the Internet goes dark, you should still be able to quickly find the knowledge that can help you survive and thrive during a crisis.

The Challenge

What would you do if the internet went down? Or even just an extended power outage? Many of us rely heavily on online resources like ChatGPT, Google, and other tools for even basic questions.

Whether it's "how do you wire up rechargeable batteries to a solar panel?" or "what is the probable cause of lower right side stomach pain?", we need access to critical information when it matters most.
